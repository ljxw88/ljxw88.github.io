I have a broad research interst, while especially focus on Bayesian Machine Learning. My previous works include variational approximate inference, graph neural networks, mutual information maximization for representation learning, human skeleton-based action recognition, etc. If you are interested, please refer to my publications and projects.

► Computer Music Enthusiast

I was enrolled in ANU Laptop Ensemble 2021, where I have been inspired a lot to be a computer music artist. I love playing the piano but I don't read music score, in fact I can't read those notes and all the composition comes from inspiration. My completely off-note practice built up my ability of live composing and transcripting. I aim to incorporate my experience into computer music composition.

The video is about my project -- a pure data based cross-platform performance interface specially designed for Novation Launchkey Mini MK3 that I developed independently. The files are available on GitHub and feel free to take a look! [link]

► Video Game Devotee

I love playing video games and watching anime, I regard them as inseparable components of my life. I own a number of game consoles, such as PS4, Xbox One X, Nintendo Switch, Xbox 360, PSV, PS2, PSP, GBSP, Subor Famicom. These seemingly rebellious gaming machine were with me for a long time in my childhood, they eased my pain and turned it into an incentive for me to be more enthusiastic and confident about my school life.
I love playing mobile games, I'm addicted to Genshin Impact (原神), Guardian Tale, and Uma Musume (ウマ娘). I've topped up uncountable stipend into such games, which actually makes me happy but painful.
I also love computer games, especially FPS games, for instance Apex Legends and Rainbow Six Siege, as well as action games such as Monster Hunter World. I prefer to play multiplayer online games with my friends rather than alone and I basically use steam as computer gaming platform, if we do share the same interest, please add me by this steam page [link].
I highly respect those console and game producers, I thank them sincerely for bringing me lots of happiness. I always believe games are not just forms of entertainment, but advanced methodologies of expressing aim-oriented flow of ideas and associations. These high immersive interactive experience are more straight-forward approach to create empathic stories, presenting to human.
Though I've lived to experience a lot of boring games, there are indeed bunch of amazing games, those you could never forget by their storylines. I was sometimes shocked by the creativity of these producers while regret that the game has not become mainstream and afraid of one day I will never see another masterpiece like this again. Gamers are thoughtful individuals, I really hope one day the society can let go of judgment and prejudice to confront the gaming community.
